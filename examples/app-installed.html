iAppLauncher

A javascript class to launch iPhone apps from a web page.

Prompts users on iOS devices that a native app is available.
Launches the app if they confirm, or takes them to the App Store to download if they don't have the app.
Cookies remember the user's choice and either launches the app automatically everytime they return to the page, or doesn't bother them again if they denied.
